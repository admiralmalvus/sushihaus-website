About Our Store / 私たちのお店について

Welcome to Sushihaus, where culinary tradition meets modern delight! Our mission is to transport you to the heart of Japan through authentic flavors and a dedication to the art of Japanese cuisine.

Founded with a passion for quality and hospitality, Sushihaus brings you the freshest ingredients and expertly prepared dishes, from our delicate sushi rolls and rich ramen bowls to our comforting udon and delightful danggo.

We pride님 on the spirit of *Omotenashi* – a unique Japanese approach to hospitality that anticipates your needs and provides a truly exceptional experience.
